Biography

Head Researcher – Volumetric Capture
Arquimea Research Center (ARC) & Rey Juan Carlos University (URJC), Spain

I am a telecommunications engineer who began my research career four years ago at ARC focusing on volumetric capture (NeRF / 3DGS). Since then I have been working toward my PhD and advancing as a researcher, currently serving as Head Researcher and leading a team of 15 specialists in volumetric capture.
